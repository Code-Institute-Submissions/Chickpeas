About the Site

Chickpeas is not just an ordinary cookbook! It is a cookbook designed with ingredients in mind. You can learn about ingredients you have possibly never heard of or even learn if there is a substitute for a paticular ingredient.

Have a food allergy? Click on that ingredient in the recipe to see if there is a substitute.

Cooking for a specific diet? See what you can replace ingredients with by clicking on that ingredient.

You can even create your own cookbook by creating recipes or copying recipes others have shared.
